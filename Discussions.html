DISCUSSIONS


Discussions should be about our Core Principles. In short our core principles are:

Economy in Service of Justice
Embracing, not fighting, cultural differences
Religious and Philosophical Harmony
Happiness

Go to Manifest for a more extensive overview of our core principles

In WordPress, you will find an opening essay. Please feel free to respond to this essay, keeping in mind that the core principles should be at the center of the discussion, preferably from different cultural perspectives. The aim is to explore these differences and see if there is a way to come closer together concerning the core principles.
